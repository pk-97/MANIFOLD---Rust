Bespoke gizmos

Feedback = a nested frame you scale & spin. Text = an XY pad with a scale ring. Edge Stretch = a direction compass + width band. The control matches the maths.

Inline modulation bands

An LFO/MOD/audio source paints a live band teal LFO, pink MOD, amber audio — across the range it sweeps, right on the slider.

Modulators as objects

A modulator carries a colour identity (PINK LFO) and lists every param it drives. Its colour is the same band you see on those sliders — trace the routing by eye.
