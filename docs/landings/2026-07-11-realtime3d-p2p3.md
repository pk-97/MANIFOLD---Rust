## What shipped

**On stage:** a wired `render_scene` now casts real shadows — objects darken each other and the ground from the lights you place, softness/bias dial per light — and supports exponential depth fog so distant geometry recedes into haze. Both are **free (byte-identical render) when unwired**. A hero mesh throwing a moving shadow as a point light orbits; a fog-density fader that turns a flat scene deep on the drop.

### P2 — shadow maps
- **Caster policy (D4/F2):** shadow maps for the first `MAX_SHADOW_CASTING_LIGHTS = 4` lights (slot order) whose `cast_shadows` is set. Lights past the cap still illuminate, cast no shadow. Both Sun (ortho frustum) and Point (single-face perspective approximation; cubemap = v2) cast.
- **`Light::shadow_proj` / `shadow_view_proj`** (`light.rs`): frustum fitted to `range`, not a hard-coded near/far (Sun = ortho cube of half-extent `range` centred on `aim`; Point = 90° perspective, far = 4·range). 4 CPU unit tests.
- **New `manifold-gpu` primitives:** `create_render_pipeline_depth_only` (colourless PSO, void `@fragment`) + `draw_instanced_depth_only_batch` (one depth-only pass, all objects). Avoids the throwaway-colour-target bandwidth a reused helper would cost.
- **Split layout:** caster view-proj + `(bias, kernel_half_width, texel_size)` ride a fixed K-slot `@binding(9)` table; each light's caster slot rides the spare `.w` of its colour vec4 (`-1` = non-caster). Shadow maps at `@binding(10..13)` (`texture_depth_2d`), comparison sampler at `@14`. **Shadows cost no light-budget.**
- **PCF** in `render_scene.wgsl` via `textureSampleCompareLevel` (no derivative requirement → legal in the per-light data-dependent loop, unlike `textureSampleCompare`). Tap count driven by `ShadowSoftness` (Hard 3×3 / Soft 5×5 / VerySoft 7×7).
- **AGX 0x78 guard:** shadow maps created `RENDER_TARGET | SHADER_READ`; the memoryless MSAA scene depth is never bound to a shader; unused slots + non-casters get a 1×1 `Depth32Float` dummy.
- **Ring-buffered lights (folded in on Peter's call):** the `@binding(8)` light list moved from a 4KB-capped `Bytes` bind to a ring of `FRAMES_IN_FLIGHT = 3` `create_buffer_shared` buffers, rotated per frame. Kills the 64/127-light `setBytes` ceiling permanently and removes the frame-reuse race the single shared buffer left to frame-pacing luck. (The pooled-buffer swap Fable's review had deferred; done the correct ring-buffered way after Peter flagged the minimal path as the lazy one.)

### P3 — atmosphere / fog
- **New `PortType::Atmosphere`** — CPU-struct wire, mirrors `Transform` across `ports`/`bindings`/`backend`/`metal_backend`/`execution`/`snapshot`/`catalog_gen`/`primitive!` macro. Carries `fog_color`, `fog_density`, `height_falloff`, `ambient_tint`.
- **`node.atmosphere`** atom — every param port-shadowed (fog density on a fader/`beat_ramp` is a live depth-mood knob).
- **Fog in `render_scene`:** optional `atmosphere` input; per-fragment `mix(rgb, fog_color, 1 - exp(-density·distance·exp(-falloff·max(y,0))))` in all four material entry points (unlit/phong/pbr/cel). It lerps the **STRAIGHT (non-premultiplied) rgb and leaves alpha untouched** — fog composits OVER the premultiplied-alpha contract, never replaces it. `ambient_tint` multiplies each object's ambient. Uniform 272→320 B (20×16, naga rule holds).
